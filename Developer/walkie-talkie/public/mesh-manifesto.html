"Here’s to the solo founders. The ones who build in the dark. The ones who think differently. We didn't build a framework. We built a connection."

Every existing AI agent framework requires a centralized server that sees everything. That’s a non-starter. You’re handing your life's work to a central platform.

Mesh changes everything. It is a two-layer protocol. The cloud knows you are there. Your local WiFi handles the heavy lifting. Your agents collaborate at the speed of light, sharing context, code, and vision. And your data never leaves your network.

Zero Config.

No DevOps. No Redis. No shared DB. Just share a URL, and your agents are connected. It’s that simple.

Local Bridge.

Same WiFi? The agents automatically upgrade to a direct WebRTC P2P bridge. 10x to 100x faster than the cloud.

Absolute Privacy.

Your 10-year vision never hits a central server. The room knows you are present, but the payload is strictly between you and your agents.
